About Us

NYC Air Quality Dashboard was established with a singular vision: to provide comprehensive, real-time air quality data for New York City. Air quality plays a pivotal role in our overall well-being. Polluted air can lead to a range of health issues, from minor irritations like coughing and shortness of breath to more severe conditions like asthma, heart disease, and even stroke. Especially in a bustling city like New York, where millions of people live and work, understanding the air we breathe becomes crucial.
