Health Impact of Air Quality

Poor air quality can lead to a myriad of health issues ranging from respiratory ailments to cardiovascular problems. This section delves into the health impacts associated with various pollutants and provides resources for further reading.

Impact of PM2.5

PM2.5 refers to particulate matter that's less than 2.5 micrometers in diameter. Extended exposure can lead to chronic respiratory and cardiovascular problems.

Impact of Ozone

Ozone, a major component of smog, can trigger asthma and reduce lung function even in healthy individuals.
